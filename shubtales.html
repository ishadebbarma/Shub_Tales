About Me

Beats, Stories
& Vibes

Hey, I'm Subarai Debbarma — a music producer, beat maker, and content creator from the beautiful Northeast of India, Tripura. I go by Shub Tales online.

Music is my language. I craft original beats, produce tracks, and document the entire creative journey on my YouTube channel — from raw ideas to polished records. Every upload is a story told through sound.

My style blends lo-fi textures, modern production, and the organic richness of Northeast Indian musical roots into something entirely my own.
